We Assume the Breach.

Unlike legacy banking institutions that rely on firewalls protecting a soft interior, our systems operate on an absolute zero-trust framework. Every endpoint telemetry packet is hostile until verified.

Session Kill-Switch™

Our core innovation. We pair your authenticated JWT session cookie with high-fidelity analytics including IP, User-Agent strings, and canvas fingerprinting.

If a bad actor steals your cookie via XSS or physical theft and attempts to use it elsewhere, our middleware drops their connection and logs the IP without destroying your legitimate active session.
